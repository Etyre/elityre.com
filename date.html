Dating Profile

What I Want

Ultimately, I’m looking for a sort of spiritual comradeship. Partership with a person who is relentlessly dedicated to making the world bright, who is striving to face up to her own demons, to better live in integrity and more efffectively be a force for light in the world, and who will help me in doing the same. I want a partner who inspires me to be better.

But for the time being, I’m just wanting more female companionship in my life; I would like to spend more time with women I like.

More specifcially, it would be great to have someone to spend time with on the order of once a week, cuddling and talking, being curious together, and maybe co-working or strength training, with a practiced expectation of being fully honest with each other.

And, especially while I'm traveling, I'm pretty glad to go on causual dates with anyone who thinks "teaching each other things that one of us understands" or "trying together to puzzle out something that neither of us understands", is a good first date, even if it doesn't seem likely to lead to any kind of relationship.
