The following are some currently outstanding bounties, related to areas that I would like to solve or substantially improve.

Note 1: Bounties are awarded for interventions that successfully solve my problem. It is a possibility that you'll suggest some intervention that matches what I describe, but for some reason or another, isn't actually feasible as a solution. I may pay partial bounties for partial solutions, at my own discretion.

Note 2: To be eligible for the full bounty, an intervention should be complete in of itself, not requiring me to do additional research work or iteration, myself. However, I'll gladly give partial bounties for suggestions, that I follow up on, and ultimately work. For example, if a person reaches out about a method to increase my typing speed, and we set up a session so that they can coach me through it, and it works, they've earned the full bounty.
